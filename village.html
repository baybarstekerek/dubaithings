Global Village

Global Village is a huge theme park with admission of only 15 AED! that's about $4. Once you're inside you are met with all of the iconic structure of every country with notable architecture in the world!

As you make your way through this huge park you will see almost every country in the world represented with its goods and cuisine readily available for the park's visitors.

Inside Global Village there is also a carnival - style theme park where you can pay to play roller coasters, visit a haunted house, try to win a toy through a mini game, or if you're up for it, get on and play the UAE's largest drop and hold on to dear life!
Additionally there is also a "Ripley's Believe It or Not!"
